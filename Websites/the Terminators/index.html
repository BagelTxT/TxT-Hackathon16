Janus: Rise/Fall

By: Two Way Games

Its up to you. . .

These days people are making many poor choices. For example, joining gangs and doing illegal activities, not knowing what that will cause. Me and my team see this and we want to fix these problems in society, and knowing how games can influence others we decided to come up with one of our own. We call it, Rise or Fall. In our game you have the choice, choose your path.

Will you RISE, Or will you FALL?

Features

  • Free Roaming
  • First-Person
  • Game affecting choices
  • Realism
  • Sports
  • Presents Alternatives to illegal actions

Benefits

  • Test your skills in real time events
  • Teaches Lessons
  • Shows by example the right way to go
  • Allows players to learn from their choices
  • Significant message to players
